I'm a PhD student at the Rutgers Department of Linguistics. My research uses computational and mathematical approaches to study fundamental questions in phonological theory.

Research interests:

  • formal language theory
  • learning/grammatical inference
  • model theory/logic
  • representation
  • tone/pitch accent
  • long-distance processes
  • South Kyungsang Korean
  • Japonic
  • Austronesian
